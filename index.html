About Me

I'm an aspiring SOC Analyst with a focused passion for Security Operations, threat detection, and incident analysis. My journey into cybersecurity is driven by a genuine curiosity about how adversaries think — and how defenders can stay one step ahead.

I have been actively building hands-on skills in security monitoring, log analysis, and incident detection through self-directed lab environments and real-world simulations. I work with tools like Wireshark, Splunk, Nmap, and Windows Event Viewer and apply frameworks like MITRE ATT&CK to understand attacker techniques and map detection strategies.

I am currently deepening my expertise in SIEM & log correlation, incident response, and Python-based security automation — skills I am eager to bring into a professional SOC environment. I am actively pursuing CompTIA Security+ certification and committed to continuous growth in the cybersecurity field.
